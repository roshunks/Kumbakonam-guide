SRI PADAIVETTI MARIAMMAN TEMPLE




About:

Location: Padaivetti Mariyamman Temple, Sakkottai, Kumbakonam,Tanjore District.

Main God:Lord Shiva

The temple is open from morning 6:00 am to noon 12:30 pm, Evening 4:00 pm to 8:30 pm.

MORE INFO

  • “Mari” in Tamil means Rain. So she is called as “Mazhaiyai Tharubaval” (Goddess who gives rain). People believe that small pox /chicken pox is spread and cured by Mariamman. During drought season if we worship this Goddess, we will be blessed with rain. It is also believed that offering porridge(kuzh) will bring rain. As the Goddess resides in Neem tree(vepamaram) she is called as “Vepilaikari”.
