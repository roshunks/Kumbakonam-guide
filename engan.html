ENGAN TEMPLE




About:The main aim of establishing this samsthan is to proliferate awareness of bhakti and its importance using Namasankeerthanam, which is considered the best way to attain salvation.

Location:Engan Murugan Temple,Kumbakonam

Main God: Lord Muruga

The temple is open from 6.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. and 4.00 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.

Significance

  • Devotees pray for restoration from impaired vision,happy wedding,child boon,job opportunities

  • It is shiva temple but importance is given to lord muruga
    The entire weight of the idol stands on a single leg of the peacock

  • It is the place where brahma workshipped lord shiva with his eight eyes

  • In the month of panguni the rays of the sun falls on the lord brahmapurishwarar for 3 days
