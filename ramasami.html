RAMASWAMY TEMPLE




About:

Location:Ramaswamy Kovil Sanathi Street, East St, Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu 612001

Main God: Lord Rama

The temple is open from 6.00 a.m. to 12.00 a.m. and from 4.00 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.

Significance

  • The Ramaswamy Temple which depicts the paintings of Ramayana

  • The entire deity is said to be made from Saligrama monolith

  • The greatness of this temple is said to be the only temple where Lord Rama and Goddess Sita are in the same platform and the Lord hanuman is playing the veena instead of reading the ramayana
