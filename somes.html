SOMESHWARAR TEMPLE




About:

Location:Big Bazaar St, Valayapettai Agraharam, Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu 612001

Main God:Someshwarar(shiva)

The temple is open from 6.30 a.m. to 12.30 a.m. and from 4.00 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.

Significance

  • The Lord in this temple is a swayambumurthy

  • It is believed that praying in this shrine brings the devotee progress in his trade and promotions in job
