SANI TEMPLE




About:This is temple is dedicated to Jupiter / Guru Baghawan; located near Kumbakonam. One of the most powerful Navagraha temples. Better to visit the temple before 8 am so that crowd is less and you can have good dharshan as well.

Location:Sivan S St, Alangudi, Tamil Nadu 612801

Main God: Abathsagewarar (Shiva), Dakshinamoorthy (Guru or Jupiter)

Significance

  • Presiding deity Aabathsahayeswarar is a Swayambulinga Murthy in the temple.

  • Lord Dakshinamurthy adored as Guru Baghwan (teacher of Dharma and Yoga sastras) has special importance in the temple and therefore, the place is regarded as Dakshinamurthy Sthala ‚Äì the holy place.
