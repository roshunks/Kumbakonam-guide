KEDHU TEMPLE




About:The main aim of establishing this samsthan is to proliferate awareness of bhakti and its importance using Namasankeerthanam, which is considered the best way to attain salvation.

Location:Kezhaperumpallam, Near Kumbakonam (Kethu), Vanagiri, Tamil Nadu 609105

Main God:Naganatha Swamy(Shiva) Sowndara Nayagi(Parvathi)

The temple is open from 6.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. and 4.00 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.

Significance

  • Poojas are performed to kethu at ragu kaalas and yamakanda kaalas to get rid of the bad effects of the planets
