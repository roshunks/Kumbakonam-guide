BANAPURISWARAR TEMPLE




About:

Location:Banadhurai, Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu 612001

Main God: Lord Shiva

The temple is open from 7.00 a.m. to 11.30 a.m. and from 5.00 p.m. to 8.00 p.m.

Significance

  • It is believed that those praying to Lord Shiva in this place would be blessed with Longevity and Fame in life.

  • According to Hindu mythology, the site become the location from where Shiva took his intention at a pot of amrita together with his bow and arrow.

  • Mahamaham' is a Hindu pageant celebrated every 12 years in the Mahamaham pond positioned in the South Indian town of Kumbakonam in Tamil Nadu, India.
