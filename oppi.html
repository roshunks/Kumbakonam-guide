OPPILIYAPPAN TEMPLE




About:

Location:Oppiliyappan koil N St, Thirunageswaram, Tamil Nadu 612204

Main God: Oppiliappan Perumal, Bhumi Devi

The temple is open from 6.00 a.m. to 1.00 p.m. and from 4.00 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.

Significance

  • This is one of the 108 divyadesas,This sthalam has no salt in prasatham for lord

  • It is believed that praying to lord oppliappan and bhuma devi ensures unity among the couples
