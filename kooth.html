KOOTHANUR TEMPLE




About:The main aim of establishing this samsthan is to proliferate awareness of bhakti and its importance using Namasankeerthanam, which is considered the best way to attain salvation.

Location:Kumbakonam Main Road, Kuttanur, Tamil Nadu 609503

Main God: Goddess Saraswathi

The temple is open from 7.30 a.m. to 11.00 p.m. and from 4.00 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.

Significance

  • Vijayadasami is the most popular festival celebrated here

  • Children are made to get her blessing by writing on rice grains before admitting them to school3
