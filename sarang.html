SARANGAPANI TEMPLE




About:

Location:Sarangapani North Madavilagam, Valayapettai Agraharam, Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu 612001

Main God:Sarangapani; (Vishnu) Komalavalli Thayar; (Lakshmi) Aravamuthar

The temple is open from 7.00 a.m. to 12.00 a.m. and from 5.00 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.

Significance

  • It is one of the divya deshams listed by the tamil poets alwars

  • It is situated on the banks of river kaveri

  • Considered as one among the pancharanga kshetrams
