RAHU TEMPLE




About:The temple is situated around 8 kms away from Kumbakonam.Though the main deity is Shiva as Nageshwara,the serpent God ,Rahu, one of the planets in Hindu zodiac,has usurped the position of main deity.Milk abhishekam is performed during Rahu Kalam every day and devotees flock here to have a glimpse of it.

Location:Thirunageswaram, Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu 612204

Main God:Naganatha Swamy(Shiva) Giri Gujambigai(Parvathi)

Significance

  • One of the 9 nava graha temples

  • Shri Naga Swamy temple is one place where lord shiva was workshipped by ragu and got relieved of curse

  • Astrologers suggest doing abisekam expecially on sundays during ragu kaalam
