CHANDRAN TEMPLE




About:

Location:Sri Kailasanathar Temple, Thingalur, Thanjavur

Main God: Chandran

Significance

  • The temple is open from 6.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. and 4.00 p.m. to 8.00 p.m.

  • This temple is noted for the importance of first rice feeding of the infant child known as Anna Prasanam.
