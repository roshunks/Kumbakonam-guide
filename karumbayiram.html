🏛 KARUMBAYIRAM TEMPLE




About:

Location:Sri Karumbayiram Pillayar temple, Kumbakonam. Thanjavur district

Main God: Lord Ganesha

The temple is open from 5.00 a.m. to 10.00 a.m. and from 3.00 p.m. to 7.00 p.m

Significance

  • It is believed as a eldest pillaiyar in kumbakonam town

  • This lord makes devotees lives as sweet as sugarcane
